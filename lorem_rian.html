Q:Why did you make the decision to work at Touchmark?
A: I moved back from eastern Washington, where I was going to school for business and music. I had no plans on working in hospitality. I was looking for a part-time job and had heard about [Touchmark]. I turned in an application, and I was hired a few days later. I honestly didn’t plan on being here for as long as I have been, but once I started working here and got to know the residents and employees, I quickly realized that this is a fantastic place to work. I had worked other jobs in the past and just never seemed to get my groove there. The employees weren’t as happy, and the managers were nowhere near as nice nor appreciative as they are here at Touchmark. With that said, 10-plus years later, I am still here and just as happy as the day I started.

Q:How long did it take you to be promoted?
A: I started out as a part-time server, and about six months later, I moved to a full-time server. During that time, I had also worked as a dishwasher covering shifts on and off when the help was needed, and I also worked as a receptionist at the front desk on the weekends. A little over a year later, I was offered a full-time cook position by the executive chef at the time. I worked as a cook for about six years. There was then a need for a front-of-the-house dining captain. I worked as the dining captain for a little over a year and was then offered the job of dining services manager. I have now been the dining services manager for a year-plus.

Q:What is your favorite part of the day?
A: I would say my favorite part of the job is the time I get to interact with the residents and their family members.

Q:What advice do you have for someone interested in Touchmark?
A: If I had any advice for someone interested in a career at Touchmark, it would be to start as soon as you can. Touchmark is a great place to work! There’s always something new and exciting taking place; no one day is the same as the next.

Q:How does Touchmark compare to other companies you worked for?
A: Touchmark is leaps and bounds above other companies I’ve worked for. Although I haven’t had a lot of jobs prior to working here, it didn’t take me long to realize that this is a great place to be. Touchmark cares about their employees and shows it in many ways that are always greatly appreciated.
