Q:Why did you make the decision to work at Touchmark?
A: When you walk into a place of employment, and you get a warm, fuzzy feeling inside, then you know it’s the right fit! That is exactly how I felt when I walked through the doors of Touchmark. There was such positive energy and warm smiling faces everywhere! You could tell by the employees’ faces that they all love their jobs! I knew I definitely wanted to be a part of this awesome group of employees!

Q:How long did it take you to be promoted?
A: I started my career at Touchmark working as a Certified Nursing Assistant. While working as a Certified Nursing Assistant, we were offered the option to get our medication aide license, and I jumped on the opportunity to expand my health care knowledge. While working as a Certified Nursing Assistant, I pursued my Licensed Practical Nurse (LPN). In 2010, I became an LPN and didn’t want to finish there, so I pursued my education to become a registered nurse. I earned my degree in July 2013 and am currently working as a nurse in assisted living.

Q:What is your favorite part of the day?
A: Interacting with residents is the most enjoyable part of my day! It is so much fun listening and reminiscing with them on a daily basis. Seeing their faces light up when you walk into their apartments makes my day!

Q:What advice do you have for someone interested in Touchmark?
A: Touchmark is a wonderful place to work! The support and guidance I received while pursuing my education was beyond words. They understood my availability while being a full-time student and working at the same time.

Q:How does Touchmark compare to other companies you have worked for?
A: The leadership and teamwork are outstanding, which provides for a fun work environment! It truly is like a family here at Touchmark; everybody is so kind, compassionate, and caring! Our mission is “To enrich people’s lives,” and Touchmark truly achieves excellence by ensuring residents live the “full life!”
